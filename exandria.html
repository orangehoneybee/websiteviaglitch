The Age of Arcanum


~163 Before the Divergence

Now that the mortals had a taste of magic and power they became audatious beleving that their arcane ablities gave proof that the Gods had no sway in their fate. They began studing magic in hopes to become as powerfull as the Gods. Many of these arcane practitioners progressed in their studdies evolving into powerfull such as Halas Lutagran or Vecna "The Whispered One". This power led to a curuption of greed conflict and naive ambition. A mage, whos name was purged from history, ceafted forbidden riets to challange The God of Death. In defeating him she took his place in the pantheon becoming the first mortal to obtain godhood. Archmage Vespin Chloras seeking a similar power sought guidence from The Betrayer Gods releasing them for his aid. It had been many years since The Betrayer Gods were imprisoned amognst many diffrent planes leaving the Gods anger and vengence to swell infecting their every thought. The emense hatered and rage blosumed into unspeakable horrors and creations beyond ones comprehention. They only wanted to see all instances of joy and peace irraticated leaving despair and suffering in their wake. The Gods found the world unscathed and well-preserved, that same vengence now morphed into a hunger to dominate once again. Turnning on Vespin making him a thrall, the Gods bore through their planes and into Exandria's. Seeking the remnants of their corrupt children they gathered in eastern Wildemont, on the plains of Xhorhas, and began their foreboding kingdom calling it Ghor Dranas.

Like a beacon for lost souls mortals began gravitating to Ghor Dranas. The lords of darkness, infesting their minds, edaciously welcoming lost desolate souls with promises of great boons and immeaserable power. Their infectious offer spread like wild fire through the hearts of power obsessed mortals of the arcane.
